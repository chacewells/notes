Cymru fy ngwlad


A vineyard entrusted to my care
Is Wales, my country
To be handed down to my children, and my children's children
As an eternal inheritance

And behold the swine rushing upon us to defile it
Now I call upon my friends
The common folk and the schoolmaster

Come to me at the breach
Stand with me in the gap
So that for the coming throne
The purity that was may be preserved

Wales, my country
